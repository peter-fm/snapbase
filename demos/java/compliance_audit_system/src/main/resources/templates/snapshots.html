About Snapshots

Snapshots in Snapbase are immutable point-in-time captures of your data that provide:

  • Compliance: Tamper-proof audit evidence
  • Change Tracking: Cell-level change detection
  • Temporal Queries: SQL across time periods
  • Rollback: Restore data to previous states
